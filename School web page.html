St. Joseph's School Gorakhnath Founded on 23rd February, 2001 had a humble beginning with five teachers and 110 students from L.K.G to 1st. The School began functioning in a tin shed on a patch of land which was surrounded by mango groves and infested with wild animals.Today the place has become a temple of learning and the school has carved out a distinct place in the education map of Eastern Uttar Pradesh. Achievements
  • Academic

    Hindi and English Academies were inaugurated in the year 2010 with the aim of developing skills of public speaking among students. These academies organize debates discussions and elocutions from time to time on topics of relevance and the students take active participation in them.

    Seminars

    Distinguished resource persons from across the country visit the campus to provide life skill training to both the students and the teachers. Orientation programs are held for the teachers annually. Various Awareness programs and personality development programs are organised for the students as well.

    Sports & Games

    A sound mind begets in a sound body only. The school gives due importance for physical fitness through regular physical training, sports and games. Annual Sports and Games Meet is held every year. Besides, students get opportunities to take part in the Inter House and Inter school competitions.The school allots games periods and physical education periods for the students with the motivation of developing healthy physique and the need for living fit and healthy. The school has facilities for Basket Ball, Cricket, Football and other track and field events. Inter House competitions are held every year for various games to develop sportsman spirit and team spirit. The students take active participation in the various inter school competitions organized and often bring laurels to the credit of the school.

    Cultural Activities

    Considering the fact that education is not only classroom teaching or merely curricular activities, the school promotes cultural, outdoor and social activities like adult literacy and social awareness campaigns, health care mobilization, planting of trees and interacting with the less privileged. The school organizes various cultural competitions and gives opportunities to the students to nurture and display their talents. We actively participate in various inter school cultural events organized by the Catholic Diocese of Gorakhpur education society, ICSE board and other organizations. Weekly cultural competitions are organized for the students for classes I- V.
